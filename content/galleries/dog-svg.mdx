## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I choose the right dog SVG style?
Consider your audience and purpose. Use realistic styles for professional veterinary sites, cartoon styles for children's products, minimalist for modern branding, and detailed for educational materials.

### Can I create specific breed representations?
Yes! Focus on distinctive features like ear shape, muzzle length, coat type, and body proportions. Our AI generator can create breed-specific designs with detailed prompts.

### What's the best way to show dog emotions?
Emphasize eyes, ear position, tail placement, and mouth shape. Happy dogs have relaxed features, alert dogs show tension, and playful dogs display dynamic poses.

### How do I make dog SVGs child-friendly?
Use rounded shapes, bright colors, exaggerated happy expressions, simplified features, and playful poses. Avoid realistic details that might seem scary to young children.

### Are dog paw prints included in collections?
Yes! Most dog SVG collections include various paw print designs, from realistic to stylized, in different sizes suitable for trails, borders, and decorative elements.
